Your starting point: Full-stack web developer at an agency. Strong in Next.js, some WordPress. Your target: AI Engineer. Your unfair advantage: You already ship production web apps. Most people learning AI can't. The fastest path to an "AI Engineer" job in 2026 is not becoming a data scientist — it's becoming the person who builds real, shipped, AI-powered products.

Build first, deepen the theory as needed. Do not spend 6 months on linear algebra and Andrew Ng's ML course before touching an LLM API — that's the classic trap that kills momentum.
